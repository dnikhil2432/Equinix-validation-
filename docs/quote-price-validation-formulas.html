Quote Price Validation – All Formulas

Invoice line (ILI) vs matched quote line (QLI). Invoice start date is taken from the invoice file only for CUP.

1. Derived unit price (when missing on ILI)

Unit Price = LLA ÷ Quantity

Used when ILI has LLA and Quantity but no Unit Price (and not both zero).

2. Derived LLA (when missing on ILI)

LLA = Unit Price × Quantity

Used when ILI has Unit Price and Quantity but LLA is empty or zero.

3. Contract Unit Price (CUP) – from invoice start date only

Uses invoice file invoice_start_date (or billing_from / service_start_date).

Date ranges:

  • endInitial = invoice_start_date + initial_term (months)
  • endFirstTerm = endInitial + term (months)
  • numCompletedTerms = floor((today − endInitial) ÷ (term × 30.44 × 24 × 60 × 60 × 1000))

CUP by period:

today < invoice_start_date ⇒ CUP = QLI Unit Price
today < endInitial ⇒ CUP = QLI Unit Price
today < endFirstTerm ⇒ CUP = QLI Unit Price × (1 + initialTermIncrement)
today ≥ endFirstTerm ⇒ CUP = QLI Unit Price × (1 + initialTermIncrement) × (1 + increment)numCompletedTerms

initialTermIncrement and increment from QLI (e.g. price_increase_percentage ÷ 100). initial_term from ILI (first_Price_increment_applicable_after). term from QLI (renewal term).

4. Prorata factor (PF)

days = max(0, (billing_till − billing_from) ÷ (24×60×60×1000)) + 1
daysInMonth = last day of month of billing_from
PF = min(1, days ÷ daysInMonth)

If billing_from or billing_till missing/invalid, PF = 1.

5. Expected Line Level Amount (ELLA)

ELLA = CUP × Quantity × PF

Quantity = ILI quantity. Used to validate ILI LLA against quote.

6. Unit price validation

Pass if: ILI Unit Price ≤ CUP × (1 + priceTolerance)

Default priceTolerance = 0.05 (5%). Fail if ILI unit price exceeds this.

7. LLA validation

Pass if: ILI LLA ≤ ELLA × (1 + priceTolerance)

Same priceTolerance as unit price (default 5%).

8. Quantity validation

Pass if: ILI Quantity ≤ QLI Quantity × (1 + qtyTolerance)

Default qtyTolerance = 0.20 (20%).

Summary – pass conditions (quote path)

Unit Price ≤ CUP × 1.05
LLA ≤ ELLA × 1.05    where ELLA = CUP × Qty × PF
Quantity ≤ QLI Quantity × 1.20
